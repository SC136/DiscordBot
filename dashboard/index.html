First Day Activation
A new member is "activated" when they understand what the community is about and why it’s a fit for them. Usually, this is reflected by them at least sending a message or exploring the server beyond the first few channels. New members who don't activate tend to remain inactive, so try to improve this.
First Day Activation Rate (Benchmark: 15% Talked)
Weekly Retention
Retention measures how many new members stick around in the server beyond their first day. To improve this number, focus on improving activation (see chart above) and making your community more active. Note: the retention line will always be 2 weeks behind the current date.
